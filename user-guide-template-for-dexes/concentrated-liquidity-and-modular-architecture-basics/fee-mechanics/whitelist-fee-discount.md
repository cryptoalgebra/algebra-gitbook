# Whitelist Fee Discount

{% hint style="info" %}
**Note for DEX Teams:**

Useful for onboarding institutional players, market makers, or known high-volume traders. Custom discounts can be set per address and per pool.

Available via **Plugins Marketplace**: [https://market.algebra.finance/plugin/whitelist-fee-discount/](https://market.algebra.finance/plugin/whitelist-fee-discount/)
{% endhint %}

### User-Oriented Overview:

If your address is part of a **whitelist**, you’ll benefit from a **lower swap fee** in eligible pools.

This is often used to support:

* High-volume traders
* Market-making partners
* Strategic partners of the DEX

These discounts encourage more frequent trades and higher volume, which benefits all users by increasing liquidity and improving pricing.

> 📣 If you're eligible, your discounted fee will be applied automatically at the time of swap.
