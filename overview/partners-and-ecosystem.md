# Partners & Ecosystem

Algebra constantly develops new collaborations, building a strong ecosystem of partners. With over 50 DEXes and 100+ protocols — including ALMs — built on top of Algebra, our network spans a wide range of innovative partnerships.

We work with advanced ALM protocols, blockchains, aggregators, cross-chain platforms, and top-tier auditors — all contributing to the robustness of our infrastructure.&#x20;

The Algebra Integral ecosystem also benefits from talented developer teams who build custom Plugins for Algebra Integral-powered DEXes, pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in DeFi.

Join the ecosystem: [https://algebra.finance/form ](https://algebra.finance/form)
